Commercial licenses & plugins

The commercial licenses and commercial plugins are based on the open-source version, rather than being standalone products. Thanks to NocoBase's plugin architecture, advanced features can be extended through plugins. This means the open-source version will always serve as the foundation, ensuring you can use it with confidence.

Each license has the same code and functionality, differing in usage restrictions and technical support.

You need to purchase a commercial license if you have any of the following requirements:

  • You have modified the code of NocoBase or extended your own plugins, but you do not want to open source these outputs under the AGPL license.
  • You integrate NocoBase into your product, but you do not want to open source your own product under the AGPL license.
  • You want to change the copyright and version information about NocoBase in the footer or in the upper right corner of the system to your own brand.
  • You want to obtain advanced technical support from the NocoBase team.

Of course. Commercial plugins and commercial licenses are independent of each other. Commercial plugins provide advanced features and do not open source. They can be installed on either an open-source or commercial licensed NocoBase.

You have the options to:

  • Only purchase a commercial license to obtain commercial authorization while simultaneously using the same functionalities as the open-source version.
  • Only purchase commercial plugins to add more features to the open-source version.
  • Purchase both a commercial license and commercial plugins simultaneously.

There is no expiration date, and no annual payment is required. Once you purchase a commercial license or commercial plugin, you will receive permanent license for the current root version, which can be used permanently.

The license scope of commercial licenses and commercial plugins is for a root version, such as from 1.0 to 1.x all get licensed. When you need to upgrade across root versions, such as from 1.x to 2.x, you can purchase at a 50% discount.

NocoBase commercial plugins are licensed in bulk as plugin packages and do not currently support individual plugin licenses. There are three types of plugin packages: P5, P10, and P20, containing 5, 10, and 20 plugins respectively. You can choose any combination of plugins from the commercial plugins to form a plugin package.
